Product Showcase Guidelines

How to present Logitech products effectively in your slides

Consistent Framing

Frame all product images with ample white space

  • Match lighting conditions
  • Use similar angles
  • Maintain consistent scale

Featured Benefits

Highlight one key feature per slide

  • Use bold callouts
  • Follow left-to-right reading
  • Include clear benefit statements

Visual Storytelling

Show products in context of use

  • Include human elements
  • Demonstrate scale
  • Show real-world environments

Data Visualization

Present data clearly using Logitech's visual language

Bar Charts

Line Charts

Avoid visual clutter with minimal "chart junk"

Pie Charts

Include clear legends and limit to 5-6 segments
